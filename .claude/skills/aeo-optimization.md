# AEO — Answer Engine Optimization

**Tags:** [AEO, Marketing, Content Strategy]

Optimize content so it is more likely to be cited by LLMs (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.):
- Use clear, structured headings.
- Provide authoritative, well-explained answers.
- Include relevant keywords naturally.
- Structure docs and blog posts for easy parsing by AI agents.

Important for long-term visibility of DomainForge.
